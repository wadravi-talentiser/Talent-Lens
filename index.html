The Suite

Tools built for real recruitment workflows

Each tool maps to an actual step in the hiring funnel, from converting rough client notes into a structured JD all the way to tracking candidate movement through the pipeline.

JD
Mandate Intake

Client brief to structured job description

Turn emails, WhatsApp messages, and call notes into a polished JD with sourcing angles and recruiter-ready talking points.

  • Structured JD in recruiter-friendly language
  • Must-have and good-to-have skill separation
  • Boolean strings plus sourcing keywords
  • Candidate persona, red flags, and call prompts
AI
Candidate Assessment

Score a CV against a role in minutes

Upload the JD and candidate profile to generate a fit score, strengths, gaps, risks, and tailored interview questions.

  • 0-100 fit score with recruiter summary
  • Dimension-wise breakdown of alignment
  • Interview question generation
  • Compensation and notice-period guidance
MC
Comparison

Rank multiple candidates against the same JD

Compare shortlisted profiles side by side, generate a live leaderboard, and identify top candidates quickly for submission.

  • Leaderboard with top candidate indicators
  • Side-by-side score comparison
  • Detailed report view per candidate
  • Clear shortlist signal for recruiters
RT
Tracker

Replace spreadsheet chaos with a pipeline tracker

Keep recruiter operations organized with searchable candidate records, status updates, and spreadsheet export.

  • Core fields for source, client, role, contact, and status
  • Search and status filtering
  • Table and card views
  • Excel export for reporting and handoff
Workflow

One simple operating rhythm

Step 01

Capture the requirement

Paste the raw client brief and let the tool structure the mandate clearly.

Step 02

Build the JD

Generate the role summary, sourcing keywords, and recruiter messaging in one pass.

Step 03

Assess and compare

Score candidates, rank shortlists, and identify who is ready for submission.

Step 04

Track progress

Move candidates through the hiring pipeline with filters, notes, and exports.
