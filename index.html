Most Used

Client Mandate Intake

Paste any raw brief — email, WhatsApp, call notes — and get a fully structured JD with sourcing keywords in seconds.

  • Structured JD (200-250 words, ready to post)
  • Boolean search string for LinkedIn & Naukri
  • Must-have vs good-to-have skills split
  • Target companies & candidate persona
  • Ready-made candidate call script
⚡

AI Candidate Assessment

Upload a JD and CV and get a full fit scorecard with dimensions, strengths, gaps, and tailored interview questions.

  • Overall fit score 0–100 with visual ring
  • 5-dimension skill breakdown with bars
  • Strengths, gaps & red flags
  • 5 AI-generated interview questions
  • Salary estimate & notice period
👥

Multi-Candidate Comparison

Set one JD, assess multiple candidates and rank them on a live leaderboard. Know instantly who to shortlist.

  • Live leaderboard ranked by AI fit score
  • Side-by-side table view
  • 🥇🥈🥉 medals for top candidates
  • Full report modal per candidate
  • Score distribution chart
📊

Recruitment Tracker

Replace your Excel sheet with a smart candidate pipeline tracker. All your standard columns, smarter filters, one-click export.

  • All standard columns (S.No, Date, Source, etc.)
  • Status pipeline: New → Placed
  • Search, filter by status & source
  • Table & card views
  • One-click Excel export (.xlsx)
Workflow
How it works
Four steps. Zero manual work.
STEP 01

Paste the brief

Copy any raw client message — email, WhatsApp, notes — directly into the Mandate Intake tool.

STEP 02

Get structured JD

AI parses the brief and generates a full JD, boolean string, and sourcing keywords instantly.

STEP 03

Assess candidates

Upload CVs against the JD. Get scored, ranked reports with interview questions in seconds.

STEP 04

Track & submit

Add shortlisted candidates to the tracker, update status, and export to Excel for client submission.
